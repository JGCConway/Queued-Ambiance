I'm a Dungeon Master of my friend groups TTRPG party. Currently a QA software engineer who is self teaching themselves how to do bigger and better projects.

This is one of those projects.


So, why did I make this?

I made this because I was tired of youtube ads popping up when I was trying to immerse my table. When doing a little research into already available options I noticed all of them either didn't do what I wanted, or they did but they required signing up and payment.

I wanted the following:

  • Free? Check.
  • No Sign Up? Check.
  • No Ads? Check.
  • Drag and Drop custom sounds? Well, that's Planned.
  • Environmental sound options with individual volume controls? Check.
  • Ability to play multiple audio clips and blend them together to create an immersive sound? Check.
